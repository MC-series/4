It is a heartwarming and feel-good coming-of-age K-drama that centers around the life of Kim Bok Joo, a female weightlifter, as she navigates the ups and downs of college, friendships, family, and first love. The series blends sports with romance, making it both motivational and emotionally resonant.
What stands out in this drama is its lighthearted and relatable portrayal of youth. Bok Joo's character is strong yet vulnerable, giving viewers a refreshing break from typical K-drama heroines. The show is often praised for its positive body image message, focusing on Bok Joo’s journey to self-acceptance as a female athlete in a male-dominated sport.
Although the plot may not be groundbreaking, the charm lies in the characters' development, their camaraderie, and the wholesome, slice-of-life storytelling. It’s a drama that can make you laugh, cry, and feel inspired, all while exploring themes of love, dreams, and self-discovery​.
